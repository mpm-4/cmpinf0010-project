# Health and Wellness in Pittsburgh

This repository analyzes Pittsburgh neighborhoods to identify the best areas for health and wellness using data-driven metrics from parks, POGOH bike stations, and farmers markets.

## Datasets

- *Pittsburgh Parks*  
  Park locations, acreage, types, and neighborhoods  
  [https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/parks1](https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/parks1) 

- *POGOH Bike Stations*  
  Station locations and dock capacities (October 2025)  
  [https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/station-locations](https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/station-locations)

- *Allegheny County Farmers Markets (2017)*  
  Market locations, neighborhoods, and coordinates  
  [https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/allegheny-county-farmers-markets-locations-2017](https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/allegheny-county-farmers-markets-locations-2017)

## Key Findings

- *Parks (Madelyn):* McKinley ranks #1 with 32 parks and 1,114 total acres
- *POGOH Bikes (Michael):* Analyzes station density and bike availability for healthy transport
- *Farmers Markets (Sofija):* Pittsburgh leads with 22 markets for fresh food access
